ABOUT US

We are a student group passionate about nuclear energy's potential to create a more sustainable future, while not compromising on our energy needs

OUR MISSION

The core mission of The Concordia Nuclear Engineering Society is to support a space where students can learn more about nuclear science and technology, empowering them to immerse themselves in this field via projects, activities, discussions, and events. Concordia Nuclear Engineering Society strives to foster a positive, inclusive, and open learning environment in which members, regardless of background or prior knowledge, can participate and make meaningful contributions.

OUR VALUES

  1. Responsible leadership and management.
  2. Open-mindedness and inclusivity.
  3. Accessibility of our activities.
  4. Improving the world through technology.
  5. Social and environmental conscience.
  6. Safety of our activities.
  7. Engineering excellence.
  8. Peaceful development of civilian nuclear technology to improve the world.
