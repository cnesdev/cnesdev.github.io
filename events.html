RTG CAD WORKSHOP

  • March 24, 2026
  • 6:00 to 8:00 pm
  • H831

An introductory Solidworks workshop hosted by the CNES and IEEE Concordia. Participants will learn what a Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generator (RTG) is and how it is used in space engineering, then complete a hands-on Solidworks CAD session to model a simplified part. No prior knowledge needed, ENCS account required to access computer lab.

Note: This is an educational CAD workshop only. No radioactive materials are involved.
