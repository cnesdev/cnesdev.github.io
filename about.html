ABOUT US

We are a student group passionate about nuclear energy's potential to create a more sustainable future, while not compromising on our energy needs

OUR MISSION

The core mission of The Concordia Nuclear Engineering Society is to support a space where students can learn more about nuclear science and technology, empowering them to immerse themselves in this field via projects, activities, discussions, and events. CNES strives to foster a positive, inclusive, and open learning environment in which members, regardless of background or prior knowledge, can participate and make meaningful contributions.
